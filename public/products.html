3eVision™ Worksites

PPE Detection, Traffic Control, Assets Management, Project Monitoring, and more.

3eVision™ Worksites is an integrated site and safety management system that is powered by Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, and Internet of Things. It enables organizations to have a clear-sighted vision of personnel, facilities, and sites without physically being onsite. Supervisors can receive automated notifications in real-time whenever an emergency situation is detected.

Workplace Safety and Health

Since June 14, 2022, the Ministry of Manpower (MOM) has introduced stiffer penalties to companies while conducting more inspections in workplaces. Such inspections may lead to demerit points, barring from tendering, and even immediate stop-work orders. With 3eVision, we can help you better manage such disruptive risks by ensuring compliance to MOM's Workplace Safety and Health (WSH) Regulations. Our solutions are designed to fulfill the requirements of government's regulation in an efficient, effective, and economic way.

Privacy and PDPA Concerns

We respect all your privacy needs and PDPA concerns. We offer localized solutions without relying on any external hosting or Cloud Service Providers, so that only you will have full control of any data collected. All raw video footage will stay on-premise, stored on a local Network Video Recorder, while only processed videos (with privacy masking applied) shall be retrievable from mobile application users via encoded and secured protocols.
