Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision Solutions

Select from a wide range of models that suits your business needs and application

PPE Detection

Automatically detect any violation of worksite requirements on Personal Protective Equipment (PPE). You can ensure all personnel are properly geared to perform high-risk tasks. Alerts will be sent to site supervisors whenever the system detects a violation

Vehicles Tracking

Track the movements of various types of vehicles at key junctions and critical roads to prevent congestions and fire hazards. Alerts will be sent to site managers whenever there is any violation at the work site

Intrusion Detection

Demarcate specific zones in each site to indicate danger areas. Any trespasses into demarcated zones will trigger immediate alerts. Users can draw and define custom areas, as well as customize different activation time of each danger zone

Posture Detection

Ensure works adopt proper postures and techniques when performing high-risk tasks such as handling heavy objects. Processed videos can be retrieved for corrective trianing purposes

Fall Detection

24/7 AI Surveillance to automatically detect various forms of accidents occuring within the facility, which can include fainting, slip and fall, choking, and being hit by falling objects.

COVID-19 Safe Management Measures

Ensure all workers comply with the COVID-19 Safe Management Measures, which include wearing facial masks and observing safe distance

Danger Detection

24/7 AI Surveillance to automatically detect various forms of danger occuring within the site, which includes fire, smoke and wet floors

Crowd Flow Tracking

Track the number of people and their movements within a facility. Spatial heat maps can be generated to analyze the crowd flow

Capture defect inspection data from a mobile device and submit reports easily using AI-assisted forms. Streamline workflow process by digitally approving requests from any mobile device. All inspection data will be tracked, monitored and sync-ed with all 3eVision systems.

Interactive Virtual Space

Connect effortlessly to the single source of truth database via any mobile device. Safety officers can remotely oversee whole site safety and receive safety reports through dashboard from various ends. It is a one place channel to retrieve all site related data, logs, and reports.

Data Warehousing & Analytics

Data Warehouse & Datalakes

Create a single source of truth for all on-site and remote stakeholders. Historical records will be automatically processed and transformed for analytical purposes. Predictive Machine Learning models can be trained using the data collected to perform predictive analytics to prevent accidents from happening.

Data Piplines for Extract, Transform, Load (ETL) Operations

Orchestrate data pipelines to automatically extract, transform and load raw data into a database on a scheduled basis. Subsequently, the data can be visualized on a cloud-host dashboard to provide real-time organization insights.
