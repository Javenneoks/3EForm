How Can AI Mitigate Major Human Factors in Injuries at Construction Sites?

Workplace injuries not only cause monetary damages to companies but also potential fatal losses to human. According to 2021 National Statistics Report on Workplace Safety and Health, the Construction Sector has been the top contributor of fatal injuries and dangerous occurrences among all industries for every single year from 2014 to 2021. To ensure workplace safety and to mitigate accidents at construction sites, it is critical for companies to understand and investigate in what potential injuries causes are. Human factor is one of the major factors that result in workplace accidents. However, this factor is often difficult to track and control closely. Together with strict compliance with WSH regulations and policies, AI & Computer Vision for Construction is a potential solution that could help mitigate potential human factors.

Major Human Factors in Injuries at Construction Sites

3E Systems research and analytics show that there are many factors concerned with human that contribute to workplace injuries in the Construction sector. The most prevalent factor is Misjudgement of Hazardous situations at the sites. Common misjudgements of hazardous situation that result in fatal and non-fatal injuries are being unaware of dangerous zones, life-endangered incidences, trespassing and so on. Other factors also include non-compliance with safety requirements and procedures, inappropriate task handling and insufficient safety policies.

How Can Companies Make Use of 3E Systems Solutions to Ensure Safety Concerned with Human Factors at Construction?

Human factors in construction injuries are subjective, however, advanced AI technologies, Computer Vision and Internet of Things that are integrated into 3E Systems Worksites AI System solution could help to track these factors and mitigate the potential injuries associated with them from an early stage.

1. AI & Computer Vision solutions for Misjudgement of hazardous situations at construction sites

3E Systems provides the following solutions with respective use cases for companies to monitor construction sites real-time and get instant alerts of any emergencies or trespasses.
