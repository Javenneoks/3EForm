3eVision™

Our flagship product, 3eVision™, is an integrated site and safety management system that is powered by Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, and Internet of Things. It enables organizations to have a clear-sighted vision of onsite personnel, facilities, and sites without physically being onsite. Supervisors can receive automated notifications in real-time whenever an emergency situation is detected
